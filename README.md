# GTM Operator Show

**Conversations with elite operators transforming go-to-market**

## About the Show

The **GTM Operator Show** features conversations with operators who are pushing the boundaries of how GTM teams work—exploring the thinking patterns and decision frameworks behind meaningful innovation.

Hosted by The Workflow Company, the show reaches ~10,000 highly curated GTM leaders on LinkedIn—practitioners and leaders at tech startups who are actively seeking better approaches to revenue operations, process design, and systematic execution.

## Topics We Cover

- Revenue operations strategy
- GTM process design and optimization
- Systematic execution frameworks
- Operator decision-making patterns
- Innovation in go-to-market teams
